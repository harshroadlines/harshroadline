Frequently asked questions

Common questions about our pan-India transport and logistics services.

Do you provide transport services across all of India?

Yes. Harsh Road Line offers pan-India truck transport including FTL, part load, outstation delivery, ODC, and container movements from our base in Navi Mumbai.

Do you provide transport in Mumbai, Thane, and Palghar?

Yes. While we serve all of India, our Navi Mumbai base allows us to handle shipments to and from Mumbai, Thane, Palghar, and surrounding regions as part of our nationwide FTL, part load, and outstation network.

What is the difference between FTL and part load?

Full Truck Load (FTL) uses a dedicated truck for your shipment. Part load shares truck space with other cargo, which is more economical when you do not need a full vehicle.

Can you handle ODC and heavy industrial cargo?

Yes. We move over-dimensional cargo (ODC) and industrial materials using trailers, open trailers, high bed, semi bed, and low bed vehicles with planning and compliance support.

How do I get a transport quote?

Call any of our phone numbers, message us on WhatsApp, or email us with your pickup location, destination, cargo type, and weight. We are available 24/7 for bookings and quotes.

What types of goods do you transport?

We move commercial goods, industrial materials, machinery, raw materials, containers, household and office items (packers & movers), and general freight. Hazardous or restricted cargo may require additional documentation — contact us to confirm.

Do you offer container transport for ports and yards?

Yes. We provide 20 ft and 40 ft container transport for port, yard, and CFS movements, along with export and import logistics support as required.

How are freight charges calculated?

Rates depend on distance, cargo type, weight or volume, vehicle type (FTL, part load, trailer, ODC), loading/unloading needs, and urgency. Share your route and shipment details for an accurate quote with no obligation.

What details are required to book a shipment?

Pickup and delivery addresses, cargo description, approximate weight or dimensions, preferred pickup date, and contact number. For ODC or industrial loads, photos or specifications help us plan the right vehicle and permits.

Do you provide warehouse and packers & movers services?

Yes. We offer warehouse handling and storage support, and packers & movers for household and office relocations including packing, loading, transport, and unloading.

Is Harsh Road Line GST registered?

Yes. Our GSTIN is 27BCRPP9898P1ZV. GST-compliant invoices can be provided for business bookings as per applicable regulations.

How long does outstation delivery take?

Transit time depends on distance, route, weather, and vehicle type. We provide an estimated delivery window at the time of booking and keep you updated on dispatch and arrival.
